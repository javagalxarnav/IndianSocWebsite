International Cultural Evening (ICE)

What is ICE?


The International Cultural Evening (ICE) is an annual event organized by the Student's Union (SU), showcasing the vibrant and captivating cultures of international student groups through mesmerizing dance performances, enchanting music, and engaging plays promising to keep you entertained throughout the night.

Traditionally held in mid-March, ICE offers a platform for international students to come together and celebrate their diverse heritages. In order to ensure high-quality performance, auditions for the Indian Society are typically conducted in February.

As proud members of the Indian Society, we have had the privilege of being part of ICE for the past 10 years. Our dedication and talent have led us to secure the winning position in both 2021 and 2022. We are excited to connect with fellow passionate dancers and performers who are eager to participate in this year's event.
